Zexiang Xu (徐泽祥)

Research Scientist at Adobe Research

zexu AT adobe.com / zexiangxu AT gmail.com

[CV] [Google Scholar]


I am a research scientist at Adobe Research. I obtained my Ph.D. at University of California, San Diego, working with Prof. Ravi Ramamoorthi. During my PhD, my research has been recognized by Adobe Research Fellowship (2019) and UCSD Chancellor’s Dissertation Medal (2021).

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, computer graphics, and machine learning. My research focus is to enable efficient and realistic 3D scene reconstruction and rendering, especially by reinventing 3D neural modules in classic 3D pipelines. My work has broadly covered 3D reconstruction, 3D generation, neural representations, view synthesis, relighting, rendering, appearance modeling, and appearance acquisition.

Adobe internship: If you are interested in interning at Adobe with me, feel free to send me an email with your research interests.
